Yevgeniy Golovchenko

About / Projects / Contact

I am a PhD student at the Department of Political Science, University of Copenhagen. My research is part of the Digital Disinformation project. I mainly focus on politics and disinformation on social media. I am greatly interested political sociology, social network analysis and computational social science.

To get an overview of my work see Projects .
