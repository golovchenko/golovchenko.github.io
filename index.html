Yevgeniy Golovchenko

I am a research assistant at the Department of Political Science, University of Copenhagen.

My PhD dissertation is about online disinformation in the context of the war in Ukraine and the tension between Russia and the West. My main research focus is politics on social media and I am greatly interested political sociology, social network analysis and computational social science.
